Act I

I

One source

Stop rebuilding the same idea in three tools. In Galleo you author the truth exactly once.

I.1 — The canonical artifact

Everything lives in one structured stack of blocks.

Headings, images, charts, tables, stats, quotes, diagrams, callouts — each one a real, first-class element. There's no master deck and three forked copies drifting apart. There is a single source, and it is the truth.

I.2 — Not slides-with-textboxes

A real layout engine sits underneath.

Galleo genuinely typesets your content for whatever it's about to become. Change a number, rewrite a line, swap an image — and the deck, the doc, and the site all update at once. One edit, everywhere.

  • — Every block re-typesets per format
  • — Prompt or import to a first draft in seconds
  • — No copy-paste between three apps, ever
I

Act II

II

Three views

The same artifact, rendered three ways. Present it, send it, publish it — without ever leaving the file.

II.1 — Switch instantly

Deck ⇄ doc ⇄ site, one click apart.

Walk a room through the deck. Send the same thing as a clean document. Publish the recap to a live web page. Three polished renderings, zero reformatting — because there was only ever one set of content.

II.2 — It re-typesets, it doesn't break

Every view looks designed — not exported.

A line that anchors a slide becomes a section heading in the document and a hero on the website. Galleo lays each block out natively for its format, so nothing reflows into a mess. You stop babysitting layouts and start shipping.

Act III

III

Make it good

A draft is the easy part now. The hard part is judgment — and that's the part Galleo is built for.

III.1 — 36 designer themes

Restyle everything in a single click.

Each theme is a complete system — a font trio, a color world, a radius language. Apply one and the entire artifact restyles across all three views, while your content stays exactly where it was. Taste, on tap.

III.2 — The thesis

The bottleneck moved from making to judging.

AI made a first draft free — and the average deck worse. When everyone can generate, the work becomes deciding what's good and fixing what isn't. Galleo is the editor for the judging: the controls to make it actually good.
