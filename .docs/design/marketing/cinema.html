Questions

Good to know.

Can I really switch between deck, doc, and site?+

Yes. There's one canonical artifact, and the deck, document, and website are live views of it. Switch instantly — an edit in any view shows up in all of them.

What can I export?+

High-fidelity, vector-crisp PDF and editable PPTX, plus one-click publishing to a live, hosted website. Every export stays faithful to what you see on screen.

How do the 36 themes work?+

Each theme is a complete design system — font trio, color, corner radius, border weight, and shadow character. Applying one restyles the entire artifact structurally, then re-typesets the layout to match.

Can my team collaborate?+

Yes — real-time collaboration, shared workspaces, and folders. Everyone works from the same source of truth, so formats never drift apart.

Who owns my content and data?+

You do. Your work is private to your workspace, exportable at any time, and never used to train shared models.
